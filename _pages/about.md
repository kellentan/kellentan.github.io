I am a second year Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) graduate student at Princeton University, working with Prof. Suma Bhat. My research interests are in data-centric natural language processing: how to generate/create data, how to train on data, and how to get insights about model behaviors through our data. I am also a strong proponent of open source - research should be reproducible and easy to understand in order to drive further development. During my graduate studies, I have been fortunate to intern at IBM Research (Summer 2024).

Previously, I was an undergraduate in Electrical Engineering at UCLA, working with Prof. Fabien Scalzo. I had the pleasure of developing ML methods for ischemic stroke diagnosis. I also completed an internship at PerfuseAI, a health startup focused on stroke imaging.
